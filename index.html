Sport

The UK stands out for the variety of its sporting interests. Sport plays an important role in promoting integration and cultivating talent in the country. The country is the birthplace of several major international sports such as football, rugby, cricket, golf, tennis and also athletics. Sport is a major source of entertainment in the UK, with thousands of spectators filling stadiums and arenas to cheer on their favourite teams. The British are very successful in many sports, for example in formula one Lewis Hamilton is a seven-time world champion, or in football, they have a strong national team.

Law

The Uk's law is a bit different than Polish. In the Uk we drive on the left side of the road, meanwhile in poland we drive on the right side. In england while we crossing a street and we jump the lights and the car will hit us this is our fault. In poland in the same situation the driver would be punished. In england a higher blood alcohol concentration is allowed while driving a road wehicle and it is 0,8 ‰. In poland it is 0,2‰, so we can see noticeable difference. In the Uk an oral agreement is sufficentt, you don't need to create a written agreement and they avoid unnesecary papers. They don't have election silence
